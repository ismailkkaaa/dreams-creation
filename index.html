Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 3-5 business days within India. For international orders, please allow 10-15 business days. Express shipping options are available at checkout.

Do you offer customization for all products?

Yes! We specialize in customization. Most of our products can be personalized with names, dates, colors, and designs of your choice. Simply mention your customization requirements in the order form.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including UPI, bank transfers, PayTM, and cash on delivery (for select areas). International customers can pay via PayPal.

What is your return policy?

We have a 7-day return policy for standard items. Custom-made items cannot be returned unless there's a manufacturing defect. Please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order if there are any issues.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email and WhatsApp. You can use this number to track your package on our website or the courier's website.
